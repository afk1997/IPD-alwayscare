A: Inline Time-Slot Buttons

Each med shows as a card with time-slot buttons below it. Green = given (shows who + when). Red = overdue. Grey = pending/future. Single tap on a slot to mark as given. Long-press to skip with reason. Everything visible at a glance.

B: Time-Grouped Checklist

Meds grouped by scheduled time (like a to-do list). Checkbox per med. Time groups show status: Completed (green), Pending, Upcoming (dimmed), Overdue (red). Reads top-to-bottom like a shift checklist. Tap checkbox = given, tap med row = skip option.
